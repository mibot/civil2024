XXVIII Ordinary
National Congress of Civil Engineers of Ecuador

October 4 - 5, 2024, Guayaquil, Ecuador

Welcome to the XXVIII Ordinary National Congress of Civil Engineers of Ecuador.

Conference

This Congress of Civil Engineers of Ecuador aims to bring together professionals, academics and students in the area to share the latest advances, research and developments in the various branches of civil engineering. This meeting seeks to promote the exchange of knowledge, experiences and best practices, as well as promote collaboration and networking among participants. It will take place at the Hotel Trip by Wyndham (Old Sonesta Hotel) located in Guayaquil, Ecuador.

The congress invites you to present research papers in both Spanish and English.

Topics:

  • Civil Construction
  • Health Worker
  • Structures
  • Hydraulics
  • Geotechnics
  • Roads
  • BIM/Lean Construction
  • Public Procurement

Call for Papers

All research works that are assigned to one or more of the thematic lines listed on this website will be welcome, from professionals, academics and students.

Research papers (scientific articles and/or state of the art) on all congress topics must be prepared according to the following guidelines.< br>
Articles that do not meet the above guidelines may be rejected without review.

Evaluation

Each contribution will be reviewed by at least three members of the Program Committee. For the acceptance decision, novelty, technical depth, elegance, practical or theoretical impact, and presentation will be considered:

  • Solidity: Articles must be supported by the rigorous application of appropriate research methods.
  • Relevance: Authors are expected to argue the relevance and usefulness of their research and discuss novelty through comparison with related works.
  • New: Contributions must be original and explained clearly and correctly with respect to the state of the art.
  • Replicability: Contributions will take into account the extent to which sufficient information is available to support total or partial independent replication of the conclusions found.
  • Quality of Presentation: Contributions are expected to meet high standards of presentation, including English language, avoidance of ambiguity, clearly legible figures and tables, and formatting instructions.
