Welcome to the First International Conference on PhD Research about Translation

Conference

This conference aims at focusing on doctoral research in the field of translation, as well as providing a forum for exchanging ideas and improving together. This encounter is created by and for PhD students, and is geared towards PhD students and people who have recently defended their thesis (within the last three years).


In addition to promoting current research in the field of translation, we want to offer a safe space for getting to know each other and learn from each other.


The conference welcomes contributions both in Spanish and English.

Lines of research:

  • Audiovisual translation
  • History of translation
  • Ideology and sociology of translation
  • Literary translation
  • Specialized translation
  • Translation and new technologies
  • Translation and society
  • Translation theory
  • Translator training

Registration and Call for Papers

We welcome presentation proposals related to one or more research lines cited on this website, from PhD students or those who have defended their PhD thesis during the last three (3) years.

  • If you wish to propose a presentation, you will need to fill out this form before October 31st 2023. You will also need to fill out the registration form.
  • If you wish to attend as a listener, you will need to fill out this form.

In order to receive an attendance certificate, you will need to be registered as a listener and fill out the signature sheet at the conference. Speakers will also receive a presentation certificate.

Information

Presentations:

  • Presentations will last up to 20 minutes and may be accompanied by a slideshow.
  • Speakers can present their work either in Spanish or English.

Fees:

  • Registration and attendance to the conference will be completely free.

Registration rights:

  • Speakers: Speakers will receive a welcome bag. Lunch expenses will be borne to each attendee, but you may book a place at the cafeteria through this form.

  • Non-speaking attendees: Each attendee will receive a welcome bag. Lunch expenses will be borne to each attendee, but you may book a place at the cafeteria through this form.
